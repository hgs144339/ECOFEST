Ticket

 

A single normal ticket which grants access to the festival for the wholde day will cost £35 for one person.This ticket means you can only stand whilst watching the acts. A seated ticket grants access to all before and will cost £50. Finally, a VIP ticket will cost £100 which will include a spot to watch the acts of your choice, special access to premium foods and a backstage pass to meet all the acts!
