How it helps the enviroment

 

Eco fest uses 100% recyled plates and cups in order to reduce the amount of plastic destroying our enviroment. All money raised goes towards the removal of plastic in the oceans and landfill sites. Last years Eco Fest helped save the lives of 17 sea turtles, 34 dolphins and 3 whales.This years contributins will go towrds saving the livesof over 50 sea lions.Not only do your purchases help save the lives of hudreds of sea and land animals, they also go to improving machinery used for recycling so more can be recycled at once.
